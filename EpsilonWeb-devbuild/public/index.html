What is Epsilon?

Epsilon Is organized by STEM, a student society at Alpha College. The event is a 3-day Science Olympiad themed on cosmology with fun modules for everyone!

What is the Epsilon Fee Structure?

You can register for Epsilon within 2 normal intake stages. Earlybird - Regular.
You can select Upto 6 modules, with a maximum of 5 individuals per delegation. Be quick as Epsilon can only accomadate a limited number of people.
